FAQs

Answers to common questions about the process, payment, and more

How do I get started

Just book a quick call so we can discuss your project, goals, and see if we're a good fit. After that, you can hire me directly through Contra

What tools do you use for communication and delivery?

Everything is handled through Contra—from messaging and updates to file delivery and payments. It keeps things organized and transparent

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary depending on the scope, but most projects take between 2–4 weeks. I’ll give you a clear timeline before we begin

What if I need help after the project is done?

I offer free support for 1 month after delivery to handle any small tweaks or questions.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, I typically work with a 50/50 structure—50% upfront, and 50% on delivery. Custom plans can be arranged for larger projects

What if I’m not sure what I need yet?

No problem! Book a call and we’ll talk it through. I’m happy to help you figure out the best direction based on your goals and budget
